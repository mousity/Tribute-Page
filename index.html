Fun Facts About Domestic Species

  • Most domestic species live a long time; Depending on the buddy you choose, they can live anywhere from 10 to over 100 years!
  • Their intelligence is about the same as a human toddler. Bigger species can learn the definitions of basic words!
  • No smell! Unlike your local pawed neighbors, bird don't smell at all. A perfect companion to keep a home clean!
  • Birds require specialized care. Despite being an amazing pet, they require a lot of time and money, as they are technically considered exotic animals.
  • Very few birds show any real sign of difference between male and female counterparts. Many people end up naming their bird "Vicky" only to find their bird was actually a "Victor" the whole time.
